        <p><strong>Dear Promoters,</strong></p>

        <p>We’re thrilled to announce that the legendary Hungarian band TORMENTOR, featuring Attila Csihar of Mayhem, will be touring the US for the first time next year. Formed in the 80s, TORMENTOR has profoundly influenced major acts such as Mayhem, Darkthrone, Gorgoroth, or Dissection. In celebration of their 40th anniversary, they will embark on this historic US tour.</p>

        <p>The band is seeking headline show opportunities anywhere in the US, ideally between <strong>March 11th and March 23rd, 2025</strong>. They already have US visas valid through August 2025.</p>

        <p>This year, TORMENTOR headlined several major festivals, including Eve of War (US) and Cosmic Void Festival (UK).</p>
